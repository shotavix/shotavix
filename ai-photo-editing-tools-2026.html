AI Photo Editing Tools Photographers Are Using in 2026

Published: April 12, 2026 by Oli Sa
In 2026, AI doesn't replace photographers—it amplifies their creativity and speed

Professional photo editing in 2026 has fundamentally shifted from "manual everything" to "AI-assisted artistry." Today's tools don't just automate tasks—they learn your style, preserve authenticity, and handle tedious operations so you can focus on creative vision . This guide covers the essential AI tools professional photographers are actually using right now, from commercial-grade portrait software to on-device privacy-focused solutions.

The 2026 Philosophy: AI isn't here to replace photographers. It's here to amplify those who evolve. Photographers who thrive will be those who embrace authenticity while leveraging technology to save time on tedious tasks .

1. Commercial-Grade AI Portrait & Batch Processing

📸 影像之匠 (Image Artisan) — Best for Professional Portrait Studios

影像之匠 is a commercial-grade AI portrait software designed specifically for professional photo studios and high-volume portrait work . Its core advantage is precision: AI deep learning technology that distinguishes skin, pores, hair, and makeup details, preserving natural texture while removing imperfections.

Unlike generic filters, it retains eyelashes, skin micro-texture, and natural skin glow—critical for commercial work where "plastic" skin is unacceptable . Built-in film-grade presets cover retro, urban, moody, and Instagram styles, with support for custom preset creation.

Batch processing handles hundreds of photos simultaneously, making it ideal for weddings, school portraits, and high-volume shoots. It supports tethered capture for seamless studio workflows .

影像之匠 transforms complex retouching into simple slider adjustments while maintaining commercial-grade quality.
Windows, macOS | Commercial licensing

2. All-in-One AI Powerhouses

2 ON1 Photo RAW 2026 — The Complete Lightroom Alternative

ON1 Photo RAW 2026 has evolved into a serious all-in-one AI editor, positioning itself as the leading Lightroom alternative . The 2026.3 update (February 2026) brought major speed improvements and restored GPU acceleration for macOS users, making AI tools like Resize AI and Generative Crop significantly faster .

Generative Crop & Resize AI

State-of-the-art super-resolution technology for enlarging images while maintaining detail

Negative Mode

Automatically invert and color-correct DSLR film scans

Masking

One-click Subject & Background Masks, improved edge quality for hair

New Creative Filters

Depth Lighting, Split Field, Double Exposure, Motion Filter

ON1's "Custom Color Labels" let you tag images with workflow status (Favorites, Needs Retouching, Client Selects), making organization intuitive . It's available as a perpetual license or subscription.

Windows, macOS | Perpetual or subscription

3 Boris FX Optics 2026 — Professional Photoshop Plugin

Boris FX Optics 2026 introduces four game-changing AI tools for Photoshop/Lightroom users :

  • Face ML: Automatically detects facial regions (eyes, lips, skin) and creates individual mattes for targeted retouching
  • Depth Map ML: Generates depth-based masks separating foreground/background for selective blur, relighting, or color adjustments
  • DeNoise ML: Machine learning noise removal that preserves detail—ideal for high-ISO shots
  • UpRes ML: AI upscaling that maintains structure and fine detail

Optics also includes new creative effects from the Sapphire 2025.5 collection (PixelSort, VHSDamage) and updated Particle Illusion with 50+ new presets . A dockable Photoshop panel streamlines workflow.

"As a portrait photographer, I never thought of myself as a digital artist until I started using Optics. Now the images I see in my head come to life without spending hours in Photoshop." — Matt Stagliano
Windows, macOS | $9/month or $149 perpetual

4 SHOTAVIXr Neo — Creative Portrait & Landscape AI

SHOTAVIXr Neo uses 3D face modeling with 286 key points for precise facial adjustments while preserving natural texture . Its AI sky replacement, object removal, and 100+ presets make it popular among wedding and creative photographers .

Windows, macOS | Subscription

3. Privacy-Focused On-Device AI

5 ACDSee Photo Studio Ultimate 2026 — No Cloud, Full Control

ACDSee Photo Studio Ultimate 2026 stands out for its privacy-first approach: all AI processing runs locally on your device, not in the cloud . This ensures sensitive client images never leave your computer.

Key 2026 additions:

  • AI Denoise: Batch-process noisy, high-ISO shots while preserving fine detail
  • AI Hair Masking: One-click hair isolation for precise color/tone adjustments
  • AI Develop Presets: Non-destructive edits that adapt to each photo individually
  • Improved AI Face Edit: Natural and unnatural hair color adjustments, Splotch Removal for skin retouching
  • JPEG XL support: Future-proof file format compatibility
  • Expanded Activity Manager: Background multi-threaded processing—import, export, resize while working
Privacy First: Unlike cloud-based platforms, ACDSee's AI features run entirely on-device, keeping image data local and avoiding reliance on remote servers—critical for photographers working with sensitive or personal images .
Windows | $149 perpetual or $8.90/month

4. AI for Upscaling & Restoration

6 Topaz Photo AI — The Professional Standard

Topaz Photo AI combines denoising, sharpening, and upscaling in one desktop app, widely used by wildlife, sports, and event photographers . It's known for preserving original look and avoiding generative hallucinations—critical when authenticity matters.

Key strengths: motion blur correction, out-of-focus recovery, high-ISO noise removal, and seamless Photoshop/Lightroom integration .

Windows, macOS | $199 perpetual or subscription

7 LetsEnhance.io & Remini — Web-Based Magic

For photographers who need quick, high-quality upscaling without desktop software, LetsEnhance.io offers multiple AI models tuned for photos, digital art, and old photo restoration . It supports up to 512 megapixels—enough for billboard prints.

Remini dominates mobile face restoration, turning blurry snapshots into HD portraits . While it leans generative (sometimes reimagining faces), it's ideal for social media and personal projects where "looking nice" outweighs strict authenticity.

Web, iOS, Android | Free tiers + subscriptions

5. Adobe's AI Ecosystem

8 Adobe Firefly & Photoshop AI Assistant

Adobe's March 2026 updates bring an AI Assistant to Photoshop (web/mobile beta) that understands natural language commands . You can type things like "remove this person," "add soft glow," or "change color to warm" and the AI executes.

New capabilities:

  • AI Markup: Draw rough lines (e.g., a flower) and AI transforms into detailed elements
  • Generative Fill/Remove now in Firefly with background adaptation
  • Integration of 25+ third-party models (Google Nano Banana 2, OpenAI, Runway Gen-4.5)

Paid Photoshop users get unlimited AI generations through April 12, 2026 .

Windows, macOS, Web, Mobile | Creative Cloud subscription

9 Photoshop Elements 2026 — Beginner-Friendly AI

Photoshop Elements 2026 brings powerful yet approachable AI tools for enthusiasts :

  • Restore Photo: Fix wear and tear on old scanned images
  • Generative AI: Type what you need and AI creates it
  • Remove distractions: Photo-bombers and stray objects with upgraded tools
  • Visual tool tips: Help beginners learn editing
Windows, macOS | $99 one-time

6. Budget & Alternative Options

10 Affinity Photo — One-Time Purchase Powerhouse

Affinity Photo offers professional-grade editing with AI-assisted features like content-aware fill, automatic lens correction, HDR merge, and panoramic stitching—all for a one-time purchase . It's a strong option for budget-conscious professionals who need full-featured editing without subscriptions.

Windows, macOS, iPad | $69.99 one-time

11 DxO PhotoLab 7 — RAW Color Perfectionists

DxO PhotoLab 7 focuses exclusively on RAW quality, with AI-driven optical corrections, noise reduction, and color calibration . Ideal for photographers who prioritize absolute image quality over creative effects.

Windows, macOS | Perpetual license
How to Choose: Consider your primary work: commercial portraits → 影像之匠 ; all-around AI editing → ON1 or Optics ; privacy-focused → ACDSee ; wildlife/sports → Topaz ; quick upscaling → LetsEnhance.io . Most offer free trials—test before committing.

The Privacy & Authenticity Factor

A major 2026 trend is on-device AI processing. ACDSee's entire suite runs locally, avoiding cloud dependency . ON1's GPU acceleration ensures fast local processing . This matters for photographers handling sensitive client work, as well as those in areas with limited connectivity.

Authenticity also matters more than ever. AI tools now prioritize preserving skin texture, hair detail, and natural lighting . The goal isn't "perfect" plastic images—it's enhancing reality without losing humanity.

Your 7-Day AI Tool Challenge

  • Day 1: Download free trials of 2–3 tools relevant to your work
  • Day 2: Test AI masking (Face ML or AI Hair Masking) on portrait shots
  • Day 3: Experiment with depth mapping and selective focus
  • Day 4: Try upscaling an older image to print resolution
  • Day 5: Batch process a series with AI presets
  • Day 6: Test generative fill/remove for distractions
  • Day 7: Compare results and choose the tool that fits your workflow

Final Thoughts

In 2026, AI tools aren't about replacing photographers—they're about handling tedious tasks so you can focus on creativity and client connection . The photographers who thrive will be those who embrace AI as a collaborator, not a crutch.

AI may generate the pixels, but your eye determines the mood . The new tools provide the "how," but you still provide the "why."

"Photographers today want to incorporate AI into their workflows without sacrificing authenticity and control." — Frank Lin, CTO, ACD Systems
Final Challenge: Pick one AI tool from this guide and use it to edit a recent shoot. Notice what it handles well—and where your eye still matters most. Your BEST edit is your NEXT edit.
