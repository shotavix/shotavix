We're SHOTAVIX.

A camera blog built by photographers, for photographers — no gatekeeping, just honest advice and a shared love for the craft.

Our mission

To demystify photography and help you create images you're proud of. Whether you're picking up your first mirrorless camera or you're a seasoned pro looking for the latest gear insights, SHOTAVIX is your trusted companion. We believe that great photography isn't about the most expensive gear—it's about vision, technique, and a willingness to learn.

For enthusiasts

We write the reviews and guides we wish we'd had when starting out. Honest, jargon‑free, and always with the reader in mind.

No fluff, just glass

Every article is tested, researched, and fact‑checked. No sponsored fluff, no hidden agendas — only gear and techniques that actually work.

Community first

We learn from you as much as you learn from us. Our comment sections and forums are spaces for respectful, helpful conversation.

Film & digital

We celebrate the whole photographic spectrum — from the latest stacked‑sensor mirrorless to the timeless joy of shooting 35mm film.
